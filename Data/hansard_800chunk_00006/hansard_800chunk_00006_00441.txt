And the said petitions were severally ordered to be referred to the consideration of the committee , appointed to take into consideration the said act.—Resolved that an humble address be presented to his majesty , that he will be graciously pleased to give directions , that there be laid before this house , copies of any commissions issued by his majesty , for the Sale of Lands in the British West India Islands , between the years 1765 and 1775 , inclusive .
